What Am I Looking At?

Short context for reading the public stream.

MEA
A multi-electrode array. This dashboard shows 4 MEAs, 32 electrodes each.
Biochip
Each MEA is grouped into four logical 8-electrode biochips.
Window
Each pushed frame contains 4096 samples, roughly 1092.3 ms of raw voltage.
Threshold
Crossings are simple voltage threshold events. No unit identity is inferred.
Center of Activity
A statistical average position weighted by crossing counts, not a biological region.
